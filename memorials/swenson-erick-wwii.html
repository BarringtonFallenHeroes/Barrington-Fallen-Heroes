Tech Sgt. Erick C. Swenson

United States Army Air Corps — World War II

Home World War II

Remembering Erick C. Swenson

Erick C. Swenson was a Barrington resident who lived at 115 Riverview Drive. Before entering military service, he was studying engineering at Rhode Island College.

Military Service

Swenson served as a Technical Sergeant and radio operator in the United States Army Air Corps. He was assigned to the 599th Bombardment Squadron, 397th Bombardment Group, Medium.

He flew aboard a B-26 Marauder bomber known as Miss Furie.

Circumstances of Death

On December 23, 1944, during the Battle of the Bulge, Tech Sgt. Swenson was killed in action when his B-26 Marauder was shot down after conducting a bombing raid against the Eller railroad bridge.

He was 27 years old.

Awards and Decorations

Tech Sgt. Swenson was awarded the Air Medal with eleven oak leaf clusters.

Burial and Memorial

Tech Sgt. Erick C. Swenson is buried in the Luxembourg American Cemetery.
