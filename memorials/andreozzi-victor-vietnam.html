Remembering Victor P. Andreozzi

Lance Corporal Victor P. Andreozzi grew up in Barrington, Rhode Island, residing with his family on Bowden Avenue. He served in the United States Marine Corps during the Vietnam War.

Barrington Connection

Andreozzi is remembered among Barrington’s fallen heroes who gave their lives in service to the United States during the Vietnam War.

Military Service

LCpl Andreozzi served with the United States Marine Corps in Vietnam during a period of intense combat operations.

Circumstances of Death

On September 21, 1967, LCpl Andreozzi was killed in action near Con Thien, South Vietnam, during hostile combat operations.

Burial and Memorial

LCpl Andreozzi is interred at Santa Maria del Campo Cemetery in Barrington, Rhode Island.
