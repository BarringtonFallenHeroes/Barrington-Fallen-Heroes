Remembering Frank S. Barnard, Jr.

Frank S. Barnard, Jr. was the son of Frank and Mary McLaughlin Barnard of Barrington, Rhode Island. The family resided at 671 County Road, and Frank also lived at 82 Middle Highway.

Barrington Connection

Frank was a congregant of Barrington Congregational Church. He attended Barrington “Peck” High School before leaving during his sophomore year to enlist in the United States Army.

Military Service

Pvt. Barnard served in the United States Army during World War II and was assigned to the 42nd Field Hospital Corps in Germany.

Circumstances of Death

On September 7, 1945, Pvt. Frank S. Barnard, Jr. died from an injury sustained in Koeffering, Germany while serving with the 42nd Field Hospital Corps.

He was 22 years old.

Burial and Memorial

Pvt. Barnard was initially interred at St. Avold Cemetery in France. His remains were later returned to Barrington for burial in Prince’s Hill Burial Ground.
