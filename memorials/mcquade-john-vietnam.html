Remembering Arthur McQuade Jr.

Arthur McQuade Jr. grew up in Barrington, Rhode Island, residing with his family at 51 Washington Road.

Barrington Connection

McQuade is remembered among Barrington’s fallen heroes who gave their lives in service to the United States during the Vietnam War.

Military Service

Arthur McQuade Jr. served with the United States Army in Vietnam during a period of intense combat operations in Southeast Asia.

Circumstances of Death

On April 29, 1968, McQuade was killed in action in Binh Duong Province, South Vietnam, during hostile combat operations.

Burial and Memorial

Arthur McQuade Jr. is interred at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Rhode Island.
