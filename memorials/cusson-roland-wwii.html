Remembering Roland O. Cusson

Roland O. Cusson resided at 391 Maple Avenue in Barrington, Rhode Island. He was the son of William and Ida (Ouellette) Cusson.

Family

Roland was the husband of Carolyn (Colosanti) Cusson and the father of Barbara Ann Cusson.

Military Service

Tech Sgt. Cusson served in the United States Army during World War II.

He was killed during the Battle of Luzon in the Philippines.

Circumstances of Death

On March 19, 1945, Tech Sgt. Roland O. Cusson was killed in action during operations on Luzon.

He was 30 years old.

Burial and Memorial

Tech Sgt. Cusson is buried at Santa Maria del Campo Cemetery in Barrington, Rhode Island.
