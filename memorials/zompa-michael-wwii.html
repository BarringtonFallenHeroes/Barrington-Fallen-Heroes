Remembering Michael Zompa

Tech Sgt. Michael Zompa was a Barrington resident whose family lived at 23 County Road. He was the brother of John T. Zompa and served in the United States Army during World War II.

Barrington Connection

Michael Zompa is remembered among Barrington’s fallen heroes who gave their lives in service to the United States during World War II.

Military Service

Zompa served as a Technical Sergeant in the United States Army. During World War II, he was aboard a B-24 bomber operating from Palm Springs Army Air Field.

Circumstances of Death

On June 29, 1944, Tech Sgt. Michael Zompa died in a non-battle aircraft accident when the B-24 bomber he was aboard crashed approximately two miles north of Conway Springs, Kansas.

He was 24 years old.

Burial and Memorial

Tech Sgt. Michael Zompa is interred at Santa Maria del Campo Cemetery in Barrington, Rhode Island.
