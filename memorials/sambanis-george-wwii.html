Remembering George Sambanis

George Sambanis was born in Greece and immigrated to the United States with his family as a child. The family settled in Barrington, Rhode Island, where he grew up and attended local schools.

Barrington Connection

Sambanis lived with his family on County Road and became part of Barrington's growing immigrant community. Friends remembered him as hardworking, loyal, and devoted to his family.

Military Service

George served as a Private First Class in the United States Army during World War II. He participated in combat operations in the European Theater as Allied forces advanced toward Germany.

Circumstances of Death

PFC Sambanis was killed in action in Germany during the final months of World War II while serving with American ground forces engaged in combat against German troops.

He was 21 years old at the time of his death.

Burial and Memorial

George Sambanis is honored among Barrington's World War II fallen. His sacrifice helped secure victory in Europe and the liberation of millions from tyranny.
