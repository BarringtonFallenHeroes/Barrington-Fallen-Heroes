Remembering Gary L. Stephenson

Gary L. Stephenson was born on November 15, 1950, and was raised by his grandparents in West Barrington, Rhode Island. They resided at 21 Pleasant Street.

Barrington Connection

One of Gary’s grandparents was deaf, and he became fluent in American Sign Language at an early age. Friends and online testimonials remember his kindness and how he would often communicate with a deaf mute patron at the Newport Creamery in Barrington.

Gary enjoyed going to Second Beach in Middletown and attending Masonic Youth Group sponsored dances with friends.

Military Service

Gary enlisted in the United States Army on his eighteenth birthday and arrived in Vietnam in June 1969.

He served as an Infantryman assigned to:

A Company, 3rd Battalion, 1st Infantry Regiment, 11th Infantry Brigade, Americal Division.

Circumstances of Death

On August 12, 1969, Gary Stephenson was killed by small arms fire during hostile combat operations in Quang Ngai Province, South Vietnam.

He was only 18 years old.

Burial and Memorial

Gary’s remains were recovered and he is interred at Barrington’s Prince’s Hill Cemetery.
