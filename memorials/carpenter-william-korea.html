Remembering William E. Carpenter III

William E. Carpenter III was born on April 27, 1931, and grew up in Barrington, Rhode Island.

Barrington Connection

Carpenter attended Barrington schools and was remembered by friends and classmates for his dedication, strong character, and patriotism.

Military Service

Carpenter enlisted in the United States Navy during the Korean War and served aboard the destroyer USS Walke (DD-723).

Circumstances of Death

On June 12, 1951, USS Walke struck a mine off the eastern coast of Korea near Wonsan Harbor.

The explosion caused catastrophic damage to the ship and resulted in the deaths of numerous sailors, including William E. Carpenter III.

Burial and Memorial

William E. Carpenter III is remembered among Barrington’s fallen heroes who gave their lives during the Korean War.
