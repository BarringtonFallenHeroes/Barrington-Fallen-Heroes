Remembering Richard Stephenson

Richard Stephenson of Barrington, Rhode Island served in the United States Army during the Vietnam War.

Barrington Connection

Richard Stephenson is remembered among Barrington’s fallen heroes who gave their lives in service to the United States during the Vietnam War.

Military Service

Stephenson served with the United States Army in Vietnam during a period of intense combat operations in Southeast Asia.
